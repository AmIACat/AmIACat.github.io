FAQ

Here at Automation Industries, we experience a lot of questions surrounding our work. In our FAQ we hope to be able to answer them!

Question Answer
What does Automation Industries do? Automation Industries automates your factories or production lines so that all your products can be manufactured more cheaply and quickly than ever before!
How to get started The process begins when you reserve a date for a meeting. During the meeting, you lay out what you need, and we lay out our requirements and pricing. Then, a contract is drawn up, and both sides sign. After that, we will follow the terms of the contract until the project is finished or the timeframe is up. If the timeframe runs out before the project is complete, you can apply for an extension.
How can I be assured quality work? Automation Industries works closely with the client to assure satisfaction with the work. Every one of our engineers and coders is certified, and have years of experience. If you are not satisfied with the final result, you can apply for a partial refund, or a full refund while the product is still in the first week. If you apply for a refund, then any and all work done by Automation Industries must be returned. This includes machinery, code, and personnel.
